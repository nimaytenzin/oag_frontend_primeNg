Welcome to Depository of Law

The Office of Attorney General 's Law Depository website provides access to authorized Bhutanese legislation and related information. This website is managed by the Office of Attorney General and aims to provide users with an easy and efficient way to access relevant legislation. All legislation published on the website is available in PDF format, suitable for hard copy printing. The website's top FAQs list is regularly updated based on user queries. If users have any questions or require assistance, they can contact the Office of Attorney General for help. The website's ongoing development is planned for the future to provide even better access to Bhutanese legislation and more features for website users. Overall, the Office of Attorney General Bhutan's Law Depository website strives to be a reliable and user-friendly resource for accessing Bhutanese legislation and related information.
